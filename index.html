Ultrasound Brain Imaging

Advancing neuroscience through high-resolution acoustic insights.

Project Overview

This research focuses on utilizing high-frequency ultrasound for non-invasive, high-resolution mapping of neural structures. By bridging the gap between computational models and physiological imaging, we aim to better understand hippocampal dynamics and synaptic signaling.
